add - 0001
sub - 0010
inc - 0011
dec - 0100
and - 0101
or -  0110
not - 0111
hlt - 1000

add - Add A and B Registers together
sub - Subtract A from B (Registers)
inc - Increment A Register
dec - Decrement A Register
and - AND A & B Registers
or -  OR A & B Registers
not - NOT A & B Registers
hlt - Halt the CPU
